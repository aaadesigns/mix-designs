Design Mix of Concrete using IS 10262: 2019

Concrete is grouped in 3 groups on the basis of its Characteristric Compressive Strength.
i) Ordinary Concrete: M10 to M20
ii) Standard Concrete: M25 to M60
iii) High Strength Concrete: M65 to M100
